Frequently Asked Questions

Below are a few of the questions I receive most often. If you don't see your question here, feel free to use the contact form above.

How quickly do you respond?

I personally read every message I receive. While response times vary depending on my schedule, I do my best to reply as soon as possible.

Can I ask questions about your books?

Absolutely. Whether you have questions about one of my books, a Bible study, or would like additional information about a resource, I would be happy to help.

Do you accept speaking invitations?

Yes. If you would like to discuss a speaking opportunity, Bible study, workshop, church event, or educational presentation, please include the details in your message.

Can I request prayer?

Yes. If you have a prayer request, you are welcome to share it. While I may not always be able to respond immediately, I will gladly remember your request in prayer.

Do you design websites?

Yes. In addition to writing, I also create websites for churches, ministries, organizations, authors, and small businesses. If you are interested in working together, I'd be happy to discuss your project.

Where can I read your books?

Many of my books and ministry resources are available throughout this website. You can also explore the growing collection from the homepage.
