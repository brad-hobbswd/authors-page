WELCOME

Books Written to Encourage Faith Through Scripture

Every book begins with prayer. Some begin with a single verse that refuses to leave my heart. Others grow from questions believers quietly ask, struggles they carry, or biblical passages that deserve a closer look.

Whether writing Christian nonfiction, biblical studies, devotionals, memoirs, or inspirational fiction, my goal remains unchanged: to proclaim Jesus Christ faithfully while encouraging readers to grow in faith, discover biblical truth, and experience the transforming grace of God.

Thank you for visiting. I hope you discover a book that meets you exactly where you are and reminds you that God's Word is still alive, still speaking, and still changing lives today.

⭐ Featured Book

Who Made You Judge?

Rediscovering Truth Without Condemnation

Jesus asked questions that still challenge believers today. Rather than encouraging criticism or silence, Who Made You Judge? explores what Scripture actually teaches about truth, correction, grace, mercy, humility, and love.

Every chapter invites readers to examine their own hearts before speaking into someone else's life, pointing continually back to the perfect example of Jesus Christ.

"Who made you judge?" is not an accusation. It is an invitation to allow Jesus to examine our own hearts first.

The Who Series

Three connected books. One continuing biblical journey through judgment, authority, and the saving work of Jesus Christ.

Featured Novel

The Substitute

When Grace Meets Brokenness

Broken people often carry stories no one else sees. The Substitute follows lives marked by addiction, shame, forgiveness, and second chances while revealing the transforming grace of Jesus Christ.

It is a story of redemption, hope, restoration, and discovering that no life is beyond God's reach.

"Sometimes addiction is not the problem.
Sometimes addiction is the substitute."

⭐ Featured Encouragement

No Weapon

Standing on the Promises of God

Inspired by Isaiah 54:17, No Weapon reminds believers that victory has never depended upon the size of the battle, but upon the faithfulness of God.

Whether facing fear, uncertainty, loss, temptation, or spiritual warfare, every chapter encourages readers to stand confidently upon the promises of Scripture and trust the God who has already gone before them.

"No weapon formed against you shall prosper."
Isaiah 54:17

📖 Featured Bible Study

When Jesus Called Her Daughter

A Journey Through Faith and Healing

Walk beside the woman who refused to stop believing. This verse by verse Bible study explores one of the most moving encounters recorded in the Gospels, revealing Christ's compassion, healing, restoration, and unwavering love.

More than a miracle story, this book reminds readers that Jesus still sees the overlooked, restores the broken, and calls His children by name.
