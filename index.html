⭐ Featured New Release

Who Made You Judge?

Rediscovering Truth Without Condemnation

Who Made You Judge? is a Scripture centered Christian book exploring judgment, discernment, mercy, grace, humility, forgiveness, restoration, and truth through the life and teachings of Jesus Christ. Written for believers who desire to balance biblical conviction with genuine compassion, it invites readers to examine their own hearts before speaking into the lives of others.

Through biblical teaching, personal reflection, and practical application, this book encourages Christians to pursue truth without pride, conviction without condemnation, and grace without compromising Scripture. Every chapter points back to the One who perfectly demonstrated both truth and love.

"Who made you judge?" is more than a question. It is an invitation to let Jesus examine our hearts before we examine someone else's.
