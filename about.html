The Story Behind The Stories

Bradley Hobbs is an author, educator, and storyteller whose work centers on faith, healing, restoration, and hope. Through books, devotionals, and creative projects, he seeks to encourage readers who have faced rejection, loss, disappointment, and difficult seasons of life.

His stories are rooted in the belief that God is still working in the lives of ordinary people. Whether writing about redemption, family, grace, or personal growth, each project is created to remind readers that their story is not finished and that hope can still be found even in life's hardest chapters.

More than entertainment, every story is an invitation to discover purpose, healing, and the transforming love of Jesus Christ.
